The *Dark Mechanics* series began with a fascination with industrial ruin — places where human engineering outlasts its own utility and takes on a strange, autonomous presence. These structures become monuments to process divorced from outcome.

*Shadow Protocol* is the fourth piece in the series. It references surveillance infrastructure specifically — the forms are abstracted enough to be ambiguous, but the title anchors the reading.
