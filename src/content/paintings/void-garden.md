*Void Garden* emerged from a late-night walk through an overgrown courtyard — the kind of place where wildness reclaims order. The tension between the cultivated and the feral is rendered in deep blacks and electric purples, with sparse points of cyan light suggesting life persisting in darkness.

The piece belongs to the *Nocturne Series*, a body of work examining liminal spaces — thresholds between states, between control and release, between the seen and the unseen.
