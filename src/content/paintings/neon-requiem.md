*Neon Requiem* was painted over three months, each session adding another layer of translucent oil. The glow effects are achieved through built-up glazes rather than any mechanical process — light emerges from within the paint itself.

The title is deliberately oxymoronic: *neon* suggests synthetic aliveness; *requiem* signals mourning. The tension between these two states is the painting's emotional core.
